Create a new project by clicking the 'New Project' button in the sidebar, then give it a name and hit 'Save'.

Step 2: Choose your data

Once you've set up your project, you can start exploring the data. Use the 'Explore' page to browse the data for a specific city, date range, or dataset.

Use the 'Download' button in the top right to export the data to a CSV file. You can choose to download the data for the current view, or for the entire dataset.
